### Key Insight Summary

Sunbird Spark is essentially Sunbird ED, but leaner — same proven learning engine and building blocks, but with the infrastructure optimised for smaller scale, the add-ons decoupled, the databases consolidated, the microservices merged, and the frontend entirely modernised. The goal is to make it viable for mid-size adopters who can't afford the DIKSHA-scale infra bill, while keeping it extensible enough to scale up when needed.
