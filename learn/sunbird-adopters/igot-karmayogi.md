# iGOT Karmayogi

**Organisation:** Government of India, Department of Personnel and Training\
**Domain:** Civil service capacity building\
**Scale:** Millions of government employees across all departments and levels of administration

iGOT Karmayogi provides structured professional development for India's civil servants. It supports competency-based learning frameworks, mandatory training programmes, and self-directed learning for government employees from village-level administrators to senior IAS officers.
