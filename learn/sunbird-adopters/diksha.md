# DIKSHA

**Organisation:** National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), Ministry of Education, India\
**Domain:** K–12 school education\
**Scale:** 260 million+ registered users · 36 states and Union Territories · 2 billion+ telemetry events per day

DIKSHA is India's national school education platform. Every school in India — and the teachers and students in them — has access to DIKSHA. The platform hosts curriculum-aligned digital content for all grades, subjects, and languages, linked to physical textbooks via QR codes.

DIKSHA is the reference deployment that proved the Sunbird architecture at national scale. The engineering decisions made for DIKSHA — the telemetry pipeline, the offline-first mobile app, the DIAL/QR code system, the multi-tenancy model — are the foundation of Sunbird Spark.
